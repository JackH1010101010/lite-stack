About LuxStay member pricing

Hotels release a portion of their unsold inventory at net rates — below their public retail price — through Closed User Group channels. These rates are legally restricted: they cannot be shown on public pages.

When you join LuxStay, you gain access to these rates on five-star hotels that are 10–30% below what public platforms show. The same hotel, same room, same dates — just a better price.
