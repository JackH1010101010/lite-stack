About last-minute 5-star hotels in London

London has over 40 five-star hotels, concentrated in four main areas: Mayfair and Knightsbridge (Claridge's, The Connaught, The Berkeley), the South Bank (Shangri-La The Shard, Sea Containers), Hyde Park and Kensington (Royal Lancaster, Royal Garden), and the Strand and Covent Garden (The Savoy, One Aldwych).

Last-minute availability in London 5-star hotels typically improves from Wednesday onwards as hotels release unsold inventory at reduced net rates. The best savings are usually on Friday check-in for a single night — business travel drops off and leisure travel hasn't fully filled the gap.

Rate parity rules mean that hotels cannot show below-retail prices on public pages. Below-retail rates are only legally available through Closed User Group (CUG) channels — member-only platforms where the price is gated behind a login. LuxStay operates as a CUG platform. Public prices shown on Booking.com, Expedia, and hotel.com are the same as or higher than LuxStay's public SSP price — member rates are lower and only visible after signing in.
