## Wholesale Rate Estimation Methodology

### Industry Benchmarks Used

The existing pricing research (research/pricing/pricing-strategy-research.md) documents the following rate structure:

| Rate Type | Typical Discount vs Rack Rate | Source |
|-----------|------------------------------|--------|
| OTA Rate to Guest | 15-25% below rack | AltexSoft, industry standard |
| CUG/Member-Only Rates | 10-20% below rack | Industry standard |
| Wholesale/Bed Bank Net Rates | 20-40% below rack | AltexSoft, LiteAPI docs |

For this analysis, I've used a **conservative 25-30% discount below public OTA rates** as the wholesale estimate, based on:
1. OTA rates are already 15-25% below rack rate
2. Wholesale rates are 20-40% below rack rate
3. The spread between OTA rate and wholesale rate is therefore approximately 15-30%
4. LiteAPI provides net rates via their API with configurable markup parameters (margin + additionalMarkup)
5. Our platform applies a 15% markup on top of the wholesale rate

**Important caveat:** These are estimates. Actual LiteAPI wholesale rates will vary by property, date, and availability. The real savings should be validated by querying the LiteAPI rates endpoint for specific properties and comparing to live OTA rates.

### How to Validate

To confirm these estimates, run the following for each hero property:
1. Query LiteAPI `/hotels/rates` endpoint for a specific date range
2. Capture the net rate returned
3. Compare against the same-date rate on Booking.com/Expedia
4. Calculate actual spread

## Competitor CUG Rate Comparison

### Secret Escapes (Free Membership, Flash Sales)

**Current London Deals (March 2026):**
- Leonardo Royal London City — available via flash sale (not ultra-luxury tier)
- Middle Eight London — 5-star Holborn hotel via flash sale
- Claims "up to 60% off" on London luxury hotels
- Running "72-hour 2026 Dates Sale" flash promotions

**Current Dubai Deals:**
- Marriott Resort Palm Jumeirah — 5-star with breakfast included
- Grand Hyatt Dubai — 5-star with breakfast, dinner dine-around, spa discount
- Raffles Dubai — 5-star with breakfast, Raffles Club access

**Key Insight:** Secret Escapes focuses on mid-to-upper luxury (Marriott, Hyatt, Raffles) rather than ultra-luxury (Burj Al Arab, One&Only). This creates a clear positioning gap — our platform can own the ultra-luxury tier that Secret Escapes largely ignores.

**Pricing Model:** Secret Escapes takes 18-22% of gross booking value. Their rates are typically 20-40% below public rates on featured properties (source: Business Model Zoo analysis).

### Voyage Privé (Free Membership, Flash Sales)

**Current Maldives Deals:**
- Claims "up to 70% off" luxury Maldives travel
- Featured properties include Cocoon Maldives 5* (Lhaviyani Atoll) and Emerald Maldives Resort & Spa 5* (Raa Atoll)
- Offers holiday packages (flights + hotel) rather than room-only

**Key Insight:** Voyage Privé bundles flights + hotel into package deals, making direct rate comparison difficult. Their discounts are calculated against a combined retail price. For room-only comparison, their effective hotel discount is likely 25-35% below rack rate.

**Positioning Gap:** Voyage Privé focuses on package deals and French/European market. Our platform can differentiate with room-only rates + global luxury coverage.

### Luxury Escapes (£249/yr Subscription)

**Current Deals:**
- Atlantis The Royal Dubai — featured in their 2026 deals
- Maldives holiday packages prominently featured
- Subscription model (LuxPlus+ at £249/year + £500 join fee)

**Key Insight:** Luxury Escapes is the closest competitor model to ours — they charge for membership and offer luxury deals. However, their £749 total first-year cost is a high barrier. Our free membership with optional premium tier creates a lower-friction entry point.
