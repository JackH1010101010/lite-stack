## Cluster 1: Maldives Honeymoon Cost & Budget Planning

**Target keywords:**
- "how much does a Maldives honeymoon cost" / "Maldives honeymoon cost 2026"
- "Maldives honeymoon budget breakdown"
- "affordable Maldives honeymoon packages"
- "Maldives honeymoon under $5000" / "under $10000"
- "Maldives honeymoon save money tips"

**Search intent:** Research — couples in planning stage, 3-6 months before travel

**Who currently ranks:** Small/medium travel blogs (30sundays.club, escapewithannualleave.com, periodicadventures.com, jetsetchristina.com, holiday.com.mv), plus Expedia packages. Notably, NO major OTA dominates this informational space. These are mostly independent travel bloggers and niche sites.
- Sources: [30sundays.club](https://www.30sundays.club/blog/how-much-does-a-maldives-honeymoon-really-cost), [HolidayVibe Maldives](https://holiday.com.mv/maldives-honeymoon/cost/), [Holafly](https://esim.holafly.com/travel-tips/cost-traveling-maldives/)

**Winnability: HIGH** — Small sites rank here. A comprehensive, data-rich guide with real pricing beats the competition.

**Recommended page format:** Ultimate guide (3,000+ words)

**Key angle:** Use our Track A pricing data to provide the most accurate 2026 cost breakdown available anywhere online. Include real rates from Waldorf Astoria, Soneva Fushi, St. Regis (our hero properties) alongside budget alternatives. Show how our platform saves $3,000-$7,000 vs OTA prices on a 5-night stay. Include seaplane/speedboat transfer costs ($870-$2,198 per adult) — a cost that most guides underestimate.

**Content structure:**
1. Total cost ranges by tier (budget/mid/luxury/ultra)
2. Accommodation cost breakdown with real 2026 rates
3. Transfer costs (seaplane vs speedboat) — the hidden expense
4. Food & dining costs (all-inclusive vs half-board vs à la carte)
5. Activities and excursions pricing
6. Hidden costs: 23-25% tax/service charges
7. Money-saving tips (including: "join member-rate platforms for 20-30% off wholesale rates")
8. Month-by-month pricing calendar
9. Sample 5-night itineraries at 3 budget levels

**Differentiation from existing content:** Our guide includes real wholesale rate data and platform-specific savings figures that no other guide has. Most competing articles use vague ranges; we use actual hotel rates from KAYAK/Momondo research.

Show real price comparisons: Booking.com vs Expedia vs our platform on hero properties. Use the data from Track A showing 19-31% savings. 4. When each method wins (last-minute = HotelTonight, points = Hyatt, wholesale = CUG platforms)
