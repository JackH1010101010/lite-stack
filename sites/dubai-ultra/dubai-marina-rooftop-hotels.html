Frequently asked questions

Can I really get below-retail rates on the Burj Al Arab? ▼
Yes. The Burj Al Arab — like all major Dubai luxury hotels — distributes a portion of its inventory through closed user group (CUG) channels at net rates. These rates are 10–30% below what Booking.com and the hotel's own website publish. As a Dubai Ultra member you access those rates directly.
How much do members save in AED? ▼
On a typical AED 3,000/night suite, members save AED 300–900 per night. On higher-category rooms the savings are larger in absolute terms. Peak season savings are typically 10–15%; last-minute off-peak savings can reach 25–30%.
Is there a minimum stay? ▼
No minimum stay is imposed by Dubai Ultra. Some hotels have their own minimum stay requirements during peak events (New Year's Eve, Dubai Shopping Festival) — we show these clearly on each hotel's listing.
Is membership really free? ▼
Yes. No subscription, no service fees layered on top of the rate. You pay the member rate directly — that's the total cost.
Which areas does Dubai Ultra cover? ▼
Palm Jumeirah, Downtown Dubai, and Dubai Marina & JBR are live now. Business Bay, DIFC, and Jumeirah Beach are being added. If there's a specific property you want to see, let us know when you join.
Is Dubai Ultra a legitimate booking platform? ▼
Absolutely. Dubai Ultra is a verified Closed User Group platform using the same hotel distribution infrastructure as Booking.com and Expedia. Every booking is confirmed directly with the hotel — you receive a confirmation number, and the hotel has your reservation in their system. We are a direct distribution channel that hotels use to fill rooms through member-only pricing.
How can you offer rates below Booking.com? ▼
Dubai's luxury hotels release a portion of their inventory at net rates through Closed User Group (CUG) channels. These rates are contractually restricted from public display. When you join Dubai Ultra (free, 30 seconds), you become a verified CUG member and unlock net rates 10–30% below what public sites can legally show. Same hotel, same suite, same dates — just a different distribution channel.
Is it safe to enter my credit card? ▼
Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe, the world's most widely used payment platform — the same provider used by Amazon, Google, and Booking.com. Your card details are encrypted with 256-bit SSL and are never stored on our servers. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified, the highest level of payment security.
