Frequently asked questions

How much do members actually save on Maldives resorts? ▼
Members typically save 10–25% compared to Booking.com or direct booking prices. On an overwater villa at $800/night, that's $80–$200 saved per night. Savings are highest during green season (May–October) when resorts discount heavily to fill capacity.
Do I need a minimum stay? ▼
No minimum stay is required for any resort listed on Maldives Escape. Many resorts do have their own policies — we show those clearly before you book — but we don't impose any additional minimum stay requirement.
Is membership free? ▼
Yes, completely free. No subscription fees, no booking fees on top of the rate. You pay the member villa rate — that's it.
Which atolls are covered? ▼
North and South Malé Atolls (closest to the airport, best for short trips) and Baa Atoll (UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, world-class snorkelling) are live now. More atolls coming soon.
What about seaplane transfers? ▼
Seaplane transfers are typically booked separately directly with the resort or through their recommended partner. We show transfer information for each resort so you can plan your full budget before booking.
Is Maldives Escape a legitimate booking platform? ▼
Yes. Maldives Escape operates as a Closed User Group (CUG) booking platform. We source resort inventory through LiteAPI — the same B2B hotel distribution network that powers Booking.com, Expedia, and Hotels.com. When you book, the resort receives your reservation directly. You get a resort confirmation number and your booking appears in the resort's system, just as it would with any major OTA.
How can you offer rates below Booking.com? ▼
Maldives resorts release a portion of their inventory at net rates through Closed User Group (CUG) channels. These rates are contractually restricted from public display. When you join Maldives Escape (free, 30 seconds), you become a verified CUG member and unlock net rates 10–25% below what public sites can legally show. Same resort, same villa, same dates — just a different distribution channel.
Is it safe to enter my credit card? ▼
Yes. All payments are processed through a PCI DSS-compliant payment gateway — the same standard used by Booking.com and Expedia. Your card details are encrypted with 256-bit SSL and are never stored on our servers. The payment form is handled by our booking partner's secure infrastructure, so your card number never touches Maldives Escape's systems.
